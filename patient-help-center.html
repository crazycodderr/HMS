Frequently Asked Questions

How do I schedule a new appointment?

To schedule a new appointment, go to the Appointments section and click on "Book New Appointment". You can then select your provider, choose an available time slot, and provide any necessary details about your visit.

How can I access my test results?

Your test results are available in the Medical Records section under "Test Results". Most results are available within 3-5 business days after your test is completed. You'll receive a notification when new results are available.

What should I do if I forgot my password?

Click on "Forgot Password" on the login page and enter your registered email address. You'll receive instructions to reset your password. If you're still having trouble, contact our support team for assistance.

How do I request a prescription refill?

Go to the Prescriptions section, find the medication you need refilled, and click "Request Refill". Your provider will review the request and send it to your pharmacy. You'll be notified when it's ready for pickup.

Is my health information secure?

Yes, we use industry-standard encryption and security measures to protect your health information. All data is stored securely and only accessible to you and your authorized care team members.
